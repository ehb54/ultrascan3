#define ALANINE_MW   71.10

(A)alanine mol. wt.

Define constants for amino acids. The data is primarily from: Cohn, E. J., and Edsall, J. T. (1943) Proteins, Amino Acids, and Peptides as Ions and Dipolar Ions. New York, Reinhold. MW values have been reduce by a water molecule (18 daltons) to represent the weight in a peptide chain
